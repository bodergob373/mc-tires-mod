# Minecraft Tire Mod 
For 1.20.1. (ignore the names)

<img width="1718" height="795" alt="modimage" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/6602ef8d-44cc-454b-9ce0-bd61f246a013" />

## Description
A Minecraft Forge mod that adds rubber, sulfur, round wheels, and porcelain.
### Items

<img width="650" height="148" alt="Screenshot 2025-12-16 003803" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/c3cefb59-6c7f-4ce4-8466-e73c373dafbf" />

- Sulfur: A resource obtained by mining or crushing sulfur deposits.
- Sulfur Powder: Obtained by crushing sulfur or splashing coal dust.
- Coal Dust: Obtained by crushing coal.
- Charcoal Dust: Obtained by crushing charcoal.
- Impure Carbon Powder: Obtained by splashing coal dust.
- Carbon Powder: An ingredient obtained by smelting impure carbon powder or charcoal dust.
- Kaolin: A resource obtained by digging up natural kaolin blocks.
- Unfired Porcelain Block: An incomplete block crafted from kaolin and sand.
- Raw Rubber: An ingredient crafted from slime balls and honey.
- Uncured Tire Rubber: A material produced by mixing raw rubber with sulfur powder and carbon powder.
- Uncured Tire Tread: An item obtained by pressing uncured tire rubber.
- Uncured Tire Rubber Block: An incomplete block crafted from uncured tire rubber.
- Uncured Tire Tread Block: An incomplete block crafted from uncured tire tread and uncured tire rubber.
- Green Tires: Different-sized incomplete items crafted from uncured tire tread, uncured tire rubber, and possibly chains (may require a mechanical crafter).
- Tires: Different-sized items made by smelting green tires. Used only for crafting wheels.
### Blocks

<img width="650" height="76" alt="Screenshot 2025-12-14 220431" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/179b8403-d896-460b-81c9-df686faef5c2" />

- Kaolin Block: A resource block similar to clay that naturally spawns underwater.
- Sulfur Deposit: A resource block that can be found in large, rare deposits.
- Porcelain Block: Made by smelting an unfired porcelain block.
- Tire Rubber Block: Made by smelting an uncured tire rubber block. Slightly elastic with moderate friction.
- Tire Tread Block: Made by smelting an uncured tire tread block. No elasticity with maximum friction.
- Wheel Rims: Different-sized round blocks crafted from shafts and iron sheets (may require a mechanical crafter). Low friction.
- Wheels: Different-sized round blocks made by deploying a tire onto a matching wheel rim. High friction. Great for physics-based vehicles.
